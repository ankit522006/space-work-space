Crew

The Space Shuttle crew varied by mission . The test flights only had two members each , the commander and Pilot , who were both qualified pilots that could fly and land the orbiter . The Orbit opeations such as experiments , pay load deplayment and EVAs were conducted by the mission specialists who were speacifically trained for intended missions and systems . A Space Shuttle Crew typically had seven astronauts ,with STS-6LA flying with eight Astranauts.
