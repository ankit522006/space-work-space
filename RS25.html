RS25 Engines

RS25 engines , also known as the space shuttle main engines (SSME) were mounted on the orbiters of fuselage in the triangular pattern . The RS25 is a staged combustion cycle cryogenic engine that used liquid oxygen and hydrogen and had a higher chamber pressure than any previous liquid rocket .
