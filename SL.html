Space Lab

Space Lab was carried within the Payload bay and allowed for scientific research while in orbit . The spacelab module contained two 2.7m (9 feet) segments that were mounted in the end of the payload bay to maintain the centre of gravity during flight . Astronauts entered the Space Labs module through 2.7m (8.72 feet) or 5.8 (18.8 feet) tuned that connected to the Airlock .
